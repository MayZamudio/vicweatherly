About

I am a Honky Tonk country/gospel singer and guitar player. I was born in Tennessee, but I've lived most of my adult life in California. I've been playing guitar since I was a kid.

I'm a big Hank Williams fan and play a lot of his songs in my performances. I am a solo artist and use no back-up musicians. My sound is pretty raw. It is like walking up to a park bench to find Hank sitting there singing, "Your Cheating Heart". My performances are filled with stories of my life, music, and the history of the songs I sing...
