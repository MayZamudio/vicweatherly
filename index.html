About

I am a Honky Tonk country/gospel singer and guitar player. I was born in Tennesse but I've lived most of my adult life in California. I've been playing guitar since I was a kid.

I'm a big Hank Williams fan and play a lot of his songs in my performances. I am a solo artist and use no back-up musicians. My sound is pretty raw. It is like walking up to a park bench to find Hank siting there singing, "Your Cheating Heart". My performances are filled with stories of my life, music, and history of the songs I sing...

I am currently performing at the Fresno County Library - Caruther's Branch on the 3rd Thursday evening of each month @ 6:00pm. I am also performing at the Oakmont and the Fairwinds on the 2nd and 3rd Saturday afternoons at 1:30pm, each month. I also play at the Valley Christian Center each month. You'll have to contact me for exact days and times.
